Shido-kan Escote Dojo

Shido-kan Escote Dojo is a Karate Club that practices an authentic style of Karate, namely, Shorin-ryu Shido-kan. It is located at San Roque, Antipolo City, Rizal, Philippines. Being a part of this club is awesome. The sensei of this club is a great teacher. Being a part of this club is like being part of a family that is always there for you. Life is not the same without training with SED especially in this time of pandemic.
